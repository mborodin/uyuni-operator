To get started, create a UyuniProvider resource pointing to your Uyuni server:

  kubectl apply -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -f - <<EOF
  apiVersion: uyuni.uyuni-project.org/v1alpha1
  kind: UyuniProvider
  metadata:
    name: my-uyuni
  spec:
    url: https://uyuni.example.com
    credentialsSecretRef:
      name: uyuni-credentials
  EOF
